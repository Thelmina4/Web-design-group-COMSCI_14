Artists

The Beatles

The Beatles were a rock band founded 1960 in Liverpool. The band's main members were John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr. The Beatles are considered as the most influential band of all time and pop music was regarded as a genre and an art form because of them.

Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson debuted at the age of six, in 1964, where he performed in The Jackson 5, which consisted of Michael and his four older brothers. Jackson started his solo career in 1971 and recieved a lot of success. So much so that he later became known as the "King of Pop".

Madonna

Madonna Louise Ciccone moved to New York City in 1978 to pursue a career in dance. She performed with the rock bands the Breakfast Club and Emmy & the Emmys. In 1983 Madonna became a solo star with her debut studio album and later was called the "Queen of Pop".

Bruno Mars

Peter Gene Hernandez, famously known as Bruno Mars, moved to Los Angeles in 2003 to pursue a musical career. He gained fame in 2009 and 2010 as a recording artist with his guest appearances on "Nothin'on You by B.o.B" and "Billionaire" by Travie McCoy. Now Bruno Mars is one of the most popular pop artists of today.
